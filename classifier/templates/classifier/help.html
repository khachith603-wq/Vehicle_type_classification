How to use VehicleIQ

1. Take a Clear Photo

For the best results, ensure the vehicle is the main subject of the photo. Avoid extreme angles or very blurry images.

2. Upload and Wait

Go to the "New Prediction" page and upload your image. The AI engine takes about 2-5 seconds to analyze the deep features of the vehicle.

3. Explore the Results

Your dashboard will show the specific model identified along with a confidence score. All results are saved privately to your account.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does it recognize every car in the world?

A: Our model recognizes over 190 of the most common and iconic vehicle models. We are constantly expanding our AI's knowledge base.

Q: Is my data private?

A: Yes! Only you can see your classification history and the images you upload.

Q: Why did it show "Error"?

A: Errors usually happen if the image format is unsupported or if the file was corrupted during upload. Try uploading a standard JPG or PNG.
