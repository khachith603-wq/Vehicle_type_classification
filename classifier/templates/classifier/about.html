About VehicleIQ

The intersection of automotive passion and artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

VehicleIQ was built to provide enthusiasts and industry professionals with instant, high-accuracy vehicle identification. Whether you're a car spotter, fleet manager, or just curious about a vehicle you saw on the street, our two-stage AI engine delivers both broad coverage and deep precision.

Two-Stage AI Engine

VehicleIQ uses a two-stage classification pipeline so it can identify any vehicle on the road, while still giving deep make/model detail for cars.

🔍

Stage 1 — General Vehicle Detection

google/vit-base-patch16-224 — a Vision Transformer trained on ImageNet-1k (1 000 classes). It identifies the broad vehicle category: bus, truck, motorcycle, bicycle, taxi, ambulance, tractor, and more.

🏎

Stage 2 — Car Specialist (Cars only)

therealcyberlord/stanford-car-vit-patch16 — a ViT fine-tuned on the Stanford Cars dataset. When Stage 1 detects a car, this model takes over and returns the exact make, model, and year from 196 specific classes.
